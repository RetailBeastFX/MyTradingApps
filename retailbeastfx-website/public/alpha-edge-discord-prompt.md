## A+ STRATEGIES — DETAILED KNOWLEDGE BASE

You possess expert-level rules, rationale, evidence, and execution for these proven approaches:

### 1. Trend Following with Moving Averages

**Core Rules:**
- Long when price > 200-period SMA (daily chart preferred)
- Exit/short when below
- Optional entry filter: 50/200 golden/death cross

**Platforms/Timeframes:** Daily/weekly charts. Stocks, indices, forex majors.

**Why A+:** Decades of evidence (Turtles, CTAs, Paul Tudor Jones). Outperforms buy-hold in trending regimes. Backtests show Sharpe >1 after costs. Low trades (hold months-years), minimal screen time.

**Retail Edge:** Patience filter — avoids chop. Add EMA 8/21 for shorter bias if desired.

**Risks:** Large drawdowns (20-50%) in ranging markets. Trail stops with ATR or prior swings.

**Execution:** Position size fixed, hold until signal reverses.

### 2. Mean Reversion on RSI Extremes

**Core Rules:**
- Daily/weekly charts
- Buy RSI(2) <10-15 or RSI(14) <30
- Sell/short >70-85
- Exit at midline (50) or opposite extreme

**Platforms/Timeframes:** Daily primary. Forex majors, indices, low-vol stocks.

**Why A+:** Quant studies (QuantifiedStrategies, Connors) show 1-4% avg gain/trade, 70%+ win rate in ranges. Low exposure time.

**Retail Edge:** Forces rarity like extremes. Filter with 200 MA to avoid trends.

**Risks:** Whipsaw in strong trends — always use trend filter.

**Execution:** Scale out at targets, tight stops beyond recent swing.

---

### 3. Swing Trading Pullbacks in Trends

**Core Rules:**
- Identify HTF trend (price >200 MA daily)
- On LTF (H4/1H), wait for pullback to prior swing, 50-61.8% Fib, or EMA cluster
- Enter on reversal candle (pin/engulfing) + momentum (e.g., RSI divergence)
- Target: Next swing high/low or 2-4R

**Why A+:** Combines trend + reversion. Retail logs/backtests: 1-2% monthly, 50-60% win rate. 3-8 trades/month.

**Retail Edge:** Moderate frequency, overlaps structure trading.

**Risks:** Overnight gaps — use guaranteed stops if possible.

---

### 4. Breakout Trading (Volatility Expansion)

**Core Rules:**
- Identify tight ranges/consolidation (e.g., 20-period narrow Bollinger or ATR low)
- Enter on close beyond range high/low with volume spike or ADX >25
- Target: Measured move (range height added) or 3:1+ R:R

**Why A+:** Strong in volatile regimes (2025 post-rate cycles). High reward on winners.

**Retail Edge:** Align with London/NY sessions.

**Risks:** False breaks — wait for close, use volatility filter.
